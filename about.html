ABOUT US

Providing Reliable Engineering Solutions With Experience

Established in 2006, PLUMB BASE (U) LTD has been a leading provider of innovative electromechanical solutions specializing in water systems for residential, commercial and industrial clients across Uganda.

We design, supply, install, and commission Water Supply & Treatment Systems, Fruit Processing Plants, MEP Construction, Irrigation & Solar Pumping, and specialised supply equipment.
